The anions of the 2-azaallylphosphonates 1 react with the a,P-unsaturated esters 2 to yield the intermediates 4'. These

numerous substrates and in the presence of a number of bases. ' The reaction is regioselective and stereospecific and on the 
basis of the formation of the kinetically more strained diastereoisorner it is possible to conclude that the cycloaddition is a 
concerted one. The thermodynarnically less strained diastereoisorners 6 and 7 are obtained from the compounds 4 and 5 
respectively when they are subjected to the action of a base in a protic media. The other epirners of 4, the compounds 8 and.9, 
have been obtained by the addition of diethylphosphite to the pyrrolines 5 and 7. The pyrrolines 5 have been reduced to the 
3-ethoxycarbonylpyrrolidines 12. The isornerization of the pyrrolines 5 with a phenyl substituent in position 5 leads to the
